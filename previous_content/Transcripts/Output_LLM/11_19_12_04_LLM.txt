"Grown Ups" is a comedy film directed by Dennis Dugan that was released in 2010. The movie stars Adam Sandler, Kevin James, Chris Rock, David Spade, and Rob Schneider as a group of childhood friends who reunite for a weekend after the death of their basketball coach. During their time together, they reminisce about their youth, confront their adult lives, and engage in various humorous antics. The film explores themes of friendship, nostalgia, and the challenges of growing up. While it received mixed reviews from critics, it enjoyed success at the box office and has a sequel titled "Grown Ups 2," released in 2013.
